## What is this?

Three cognitive operating systems, each distilled from a Chinese master:

| Skill | Master | Framework | Best for |
|-------|--------|-----------|----------|
| [`/pu-songling`](./pu-songling/) | 蒲松龄 Pu Songling | Strange-as-truth × Outsider gaze × Accumulation | Supernatural narratives, social critique through metaphor, uncanny worldbuilding |
| [`/chen-kaige`](./chen-kaige/) | 陈凯歌 Chen Kaige | Historical conscience × Tragic aesthetics × Intellectual self-questioning | Epic screenwriting, cultural identity, moral complexity |
| [`/zhang-yimou`](./zhang-yimou/) | 张艺谋 Zhang Yimou | Visual-as-narrative × Minimalist aesthetics × Compromise-as-survival | AI video prompting, color-driven storytelling, spectacle design |

---

## Who is this for?

**AI video creators** making culture-driven content (think: 逃离大英博物馆 / Escape from the British Museum, Republican-era aesthetics, Chinese opera horror × infinite-flow genres, Farewell My Concubine-style visual reconstruction)

**Screenwriters & game designers** building worlds with historical and mythological depth

**AI image prompt engineers** who want Zhang Yimou's color language baked into their prompts

**Cultural IP developers** turning folklore and history into modern narratives

---

## Why these three together?

They cover three distinct layers of narrative craft:

```
Pu Songling  →  the WORLD  (strange, layered, socially true)
Chen Kaige   →  the ARC    (tragic, historically grounded, morally weighted)
Zhang Yimou  →  the IMAGE  (visual, minimal, burns into memory)
```
